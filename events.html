Sea & Valley 2026

The Vancouver Coins, Stamps & Collectibles Show 2026

Location: Nikkei National Museum & Cultural Centre, 6688 Southoaks Crescent, Burnaby

This show will feature 40 tables of coins, stamps, and collectables. Buy, sell, or trade items such as Canadian coins, world coins, ancient coins, stamps, art, covers, postcards, medals, tokens, bank notes, and more. Free coins for kids and free appraisals are available.

Show Dates for 2026:

  • Saturday, February 7th, 2026
  • Saturday, May 9th, 2026
  • Saturday, September 12th, 2026
  • Saturday, November 21st, 2026

Hours: Saturdays, 9:30 am - 3:30 pm

Admission: $3 per person (under age 16 FREE)

Note: The show will adhere to all COVID-19 rules and restrictions as prescribed by Federal and Provincial authorities. Please stay home if you are not feeling well.
