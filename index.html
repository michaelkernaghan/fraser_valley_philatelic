About the Bluenose Stamp

The 1929 Bluenose stamp is one of Canada's most famous stamps, featuring the iconic schooner known for its unbeaten speed and beauty. This stamp is celebrated not only for its intricate design but also as a symbol of Canadian maritime pride.
