Welcome to Our Society

The Fraser Valley Philatelic Society has been a cornerstone of the local philatelic community since 1954, fostering an appreciation for stamp collecting and postal history among enthusiasts of all ages.

Our History

Founded by a small group of passionate collectors, our society has grown to include hundreds of members across the region. We've hosted numerous exhibitions and have been an integral part of national and international philatelic events.

Our Mission

To promote the study of philately, support the preservation of philatelic materials, and provide a community for collectors to share their passion and knowledge.
