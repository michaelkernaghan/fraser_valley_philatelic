Regular Meetings

The Fraser Valley Philatelic Club meets typically on the second Tuesday of each month, except July and August, at 6:00 PM in the school library at W.J. Mouat Secondary School, 32355 Mouat Drive, Abbotsford, BC.

At each meeting, there is active trading as well as a silent auction and a buy board with lots submitted by members.

People start arriving at 6:00 PM, the table (silent) auction closes at 7:30 PM, then we have a short business meeting after that.

At the November meeting, there is an annual call auction with approximately 100 lots varying in value from $5 to $250.
